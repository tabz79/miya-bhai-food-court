The Miya Bhai Legacy

Founded in the heart of Old City Hyderabad, Miya Bhai Food Court began as a humble eatery serving authentic Nizami cuisine to locals and travelers alike. Our recipes, passed down through generations, represent the true essence of Hyderabadi culinary artistry.

From our signature Dum Biryani cooked in traditional bhaghar to our perfectly spiced tandoori delicacies, every dish tells a story of heritage, passion, and unwavering commitment to authenticity. Today, with multiple branches across the city, we continue to serve the same royal flavors that made us legendary.
